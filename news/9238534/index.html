INFO Spotify Verified Accounts

Difference between a verified Spotify user account compared to artist profile.

Verified Spotify user profiles:

Verified Spotify user profiles are for official businesses that Spotify recommend. Music artist profiles will not be Verified.

Verified Spotify artist profiles:

Spotify artist profiles must be claimed by the artist.
Claiming your artist profile:
Go to Spotify for artists
Choose Artist Team then enter your artist name.
Then enter details, so we/ and/ or Spotify can verify it's really you.

Once a profile is claimed it cannot be claimed again.
