INFO About YouTube Management Tools.

YouTube Content ID

YouTube Content ID is an tool that is given to YouTube Partners or content creators who have joined our YouTube Network allowing use of Content ID + YT Rights Management.

Content ID


Content ID is YouTube's copyright detector, if a copyrighted song is detected the video's settings (Cards, Ending Screen, Fundraisings, Location Restrictions and Visibilty will be forced to the artist's settings, Managing your Content ID Settings can be found on the artist account page.


YouTube Rights Management


YouTube Rights Management is basicly Copyright is that allows the channel to add their own content to protect. To get this tool you can request to join our YT Network in the artist account page. (Abuse of this system may result in an termination from YouTube and BG Records)
